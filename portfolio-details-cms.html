Multifunctional Combat Management System

A futuristic command console system designed for warships, integrating advanced technology and ergonomic principles to meet the demanding requirements of modern naval operations.

The project involved the complete development of a combat management console from the ground up, with a focus on innovative and future-ready design. Starting from initial concepts, the design evolved significantly through detailed refinement, ensuring alignment with the latest technological trends and operational needs.

Displacement (Beam & Shell)

Max : 108mm / 10.8cm

Min : 1e-30 mm / 1e-32 cm

Conclusion: Maximum displacement occurs at the beam supporting the sail.

The primary challenge was to create a console that not only incorporated cutting-edge technology but also met strict military standards for durability, usability, and ergonomics.

The development process adopted an iterative design approach using SolidWorks for 3D modeling and Finite Element Analysis (FEA) to ensure structural integrity and performance under operational conditions.

Key Features

  • Futuristic and ergonomic design for naval use
  • compliant with strict military standards
  • Designed in SolidWorks with FEA simulations
  • casing from carbon sheet metal
  • Frame from hollow galvanized steel
