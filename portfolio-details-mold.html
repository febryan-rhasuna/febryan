Mold Base Shirt Button

A precision-engineered mold base for shirt button production, equipped with an integrated cooling system to optimize manufacturing speed and product quality.

This mold system is designed for the mass production of shirt buttons, ensuring flawless filling, efficient cooling, and minimal defects. By using optimized mold flow simulations, the design achieves uniform part filling, precise pressure control, and reduced production cycle time.

Traditional shirt button molding can suffer from uneven filling, long cooling times, and defects such as sink marksâ€”slowing production and increasing waste. Achieving consistent quality in mass production requires precise mold design and thermal management.

A custom-designed mold flow system with an integrated cooling network, calculated runner and sprue dimensions, and a 1-degree draft angle for easy part release. Flow simulations guided the optimization of filling time, cooling efficiency, and defect prevention.

Key Features

  • Optimized Mold Flow Simulation
  • Integrated Cooling System
  • Precision Runner and Sprue Design
  • 1-Degree Draft Angle
  • Defect Reduction
  • Core and Cavity Insert Placement
