General Arrangement Drawing & fabrication

A technical drafting and fabrication project for centrifugal pump baseframes and components, including detailed General Arrangement Drawings (GAD) to guide manufacturing and machining processes.

This project, conducted during an internship at PT KSB Indonesia, involved creating fabrication drawings for key pump components such as the Canal 100 baseframe, coupling guard, bore coupling (N-EUPEX Type H with key-way), and impeller trimming. The drawings served as precise production references for machining, welding, and assembly operations, ensuring compliance with industrial and engineering standards.

To produce detailed and accurate fabrication drawings that meet strict dimensional, tolerance, and alignment requirements while ensuring structural integrity, vibration damping, and ease of assembly for industrial pump systems.

Using precise engineering design workflows, including CAD modeling and calculation, the project delivered production-ready drawings. These included exact dimensions, material specifications, and manufacturing tolerances. The process incorporated design, machining, assembly, and functional trial stages to ensure the final product met operational and quality standards.

Key Features

  • Fabrication drawings pump baseframe.
  • Integration of vibration-damping and alignment features/li>
  • Design of coupling guard and bore coupling with key-way
  • Carbon steel material selection
  • fine/coarse tolerances.
  • Compliance with industrial machining and welding standards.
